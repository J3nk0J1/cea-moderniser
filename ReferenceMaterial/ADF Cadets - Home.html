Check Your Compliance Currency

Due to known issues with system reminder emails, adult volunteers are encouraged to manually verify their compliance currency and take action to remediate any items that are approaching expiry. Do not rely on system notifications — check your tasks and records directly.

Instrument of Acceptance (IOA) Confirm your IOA is not expiring. Check your tasks and take action if it is due for renewal.
First Aid Certificate (FAC) Confirm your FAC remains current. Check your accomplishments and take action if it is due to expire.
Primary Appointments Adult volunteers holding unit appointments with task approval responsibilities should confirm their primary appointment remains current.
!
Recommended Action: Add Your Mobile
To maintain access, all users must register for Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA), however we strongly recommend that you link a mobile phone number to your profile. Adding your mobile phone number will provide an alternate authentication method.
