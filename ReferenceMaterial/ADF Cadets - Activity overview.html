 112470: Woden RSL ANZAC and Peace Ceremony (09:30 - 13:00 24 Apr 2026)
Status: Pending Status: Pending
(09:30 - 13:00 24 Apr 2026)

About the activity

This activity is linked to : () .

241ACU North Canberra will be conducting a Catafalque Party (no weapons) on behalf of the Woden RSL Sub-Branch and local schools on Friday 24 April 2026 within the confines of Eddison Park in the Woden Town Centre. The activity will comprise six cadets, one guard commander, two reserves and two supervising Adult Cadet Staff. The activity will be the culmination of ceremonial training conducted during unit parade nights leading up to ANZAC Day 2026. Cadets and staff will arrive at Eddison Park no later than 1000hrs on Friday 24 April 2026. The cadets will conduct two rehearsals at the ceremonial site prior to the start of the actual ANZAC Day ceremony at 1030hrs. The ceremony is expected to conclude by 1130hrs where the cadets will be released into the care of their parents/carers. All movements to and from the activity will be their own means and there will no rations provided by the AAC or the RSL. The temperature is expected to be a low 5 degrees centigrade up to 18 degrees and DPCU jumpers will be compulsory for all cadets and staff to wear.
